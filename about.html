About

A young brand, tools built with care.

Tesserae Audio started from a simple idea: build audio plugins that hold up in real studio conditions, without chasing feature lists for their own sake.

The name comes from tesserae - the small mosaic fragments that, assembled with precision, form a coherent image. That's exactly what a signal processing tool does: break audio down, work on it, then rebuild it.

Sound quality and stability first. Features come after, driven by real-world use - not to check a box.

Tesserae Audio is still a young brand. AQ-1, our parametric equalizer, is the first tool in the lineup - not the last. Every future plugin will share the same visual identity, the same conventions, and the same starting bar.
